About Mr. Entrepreneur

Mr. Entrepreneur is a marketplace-style directory designed to help local vendors and small shops quickly find manufacturers and suppliers tailored to their sector. The site's goal is to reduce friction in sourcing — whether you run a small bakery, a furniture store, an electronics kiosk, or a farm supplies outlet.

This demo provides a curated list of common business sectors (Food & Beverage, Fashion & Clothing, Electronics, Construction, Beauty & Personal Care, Hospitality & Catering, Agriculture, Furniture, Automotive, and Health & Medical Supplies) and sample supplier names to help vendors get started.

The platform is a front-end demonstration. To make it production-ready, consider adding a backend, secure authentication, supplier details pages, ratings & reviews, contact forms, and a messaging system.
